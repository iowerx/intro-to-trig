# Trigonometric Functions

Welcome to the intelligent textbook on **Trigonometric Functions**!

This course is designed for high school students to master the fundamental concepts of trigonometry, including right triangles, the Pythagorean theorem, and the trigonometric ratios (sine, cosine, and tangent).

## What You'll Learn

Through this comprehensive course, you will:

- Understand the relationships between angles and sides in right triangles
- Apply trigonometric functions to solve real-world problems
- Develop critical thinking skills in mathematical problem-solving
- Create and analyze complex trigonometric scenarios
